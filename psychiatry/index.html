Psychiatry

Aug 19, 2021
thoughts

Only psychiatrists will ask you if you want to go from dose X to dose Y of some given drug. Who should be doing the asking? If years of medical school are doing the asking, something is wrong.

Only psychiatrists will chastise you for giving the “wrong” response, because they weren’t asking you, they were giving you the illusion of free will by offering the opportunity to show alignment with their opinion.

Only psychiatrists have the culture-normalized authority to handcuff you with (profoundly flawed) substances that surmise life-long or decade-long dependency, only to flip the tables on a whim and decide that you’re an addict, or even a criminal.

Only psychiatrists will confidently state flimsy hypotheses as objective truths, such as “serotonin deficiency is the cause of Major Depressive Disorder”, and scold you for challenging them.

Only psychiatrists will happily permanently alter a child’s neurological development, because the child ostensibly suffers from a disorder, the main premise of which is failure to thrive in societal systems that humans are not evolved to thrive in. Not sitting still at one’s desk is a symptom of a widespread disorder, the DSM will tell you.

Only psychiatrists can prescribe you a “course of treatment” that can permanently leave you soulless, joyless, and perhaps even unrecognizable to your own self.

Only psychiatrists can use your verbalized self-assessment and self-reporting against you to assert complete authority over your freedom and humanity.
